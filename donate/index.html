Thank You For Your Support

Our Lord told us in the Gospel of Matthew that the “The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few.” Indeed, as we work together to support and defend the teaching efforts of the Church, we certainly welcome your much needed help. We ask first and foremost for your prayers and point you to the CUF Prayer. Then, if you are able and willing to support the work of Catholics United for the Faith financially, we welcome your generous gift.

Your thoughtful and generous support is greatly appreciated and helps ensures the long-term viability of this apostolate to support, defend, and advance the efforts of the Teaching Church.

Recurring Donations

Due to the nature of processing recurring donations, we ask those wishing to set-up a recurring donation to please call us at 1-740-283-2484. We value your financial support and take protecting your privacy seriously.

Donations by Check

Checks should be made out to Catholics United for the Faith and mailed to:

  • Catholics United for the Faith
  • 85882 Water Works Rd.
  • Hopedale, OH 43910

Refunds & Receipts

If you need a refund of your donation for any reason, please contact us within 30 days of your donation and we would be happy to provide you a full refund.

Should you need a receipt for a donation please call the office at 1-740-283-2484.
