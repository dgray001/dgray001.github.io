Thank You For Your Support

“The harvest indeed is great, but the labourers are few” (Mt. 9:37, DRV).

“For the scripture saith: Thou shalt not muzzle the ox that treadeth out the corn: and, The labourer is worthy of his reward” (1 Tim. 5:18, DRV).

As we work to support, advance, and defend the teaching efforts of the Church, we welcome your much needed help. We ask first that you consider joining us a laborer in the vineyard and become a member or start a chapter.

If you are able and willing to support CUF financially, we welcome your generous gift. Your support helps ensure CUF’s long-term viability. You can use the form below to make a donation on-line.

Most importantly, we ask for your prayers. We know that without God’s grace, our greatest efforts will have no effect. To that end, we point you to the CUF Prayer.

Recurring Donations
