What is Information Services?

Information Services is the backbone of CUF!

From its beginnings, CUF provided answers to any questions about the Catholic Church; questions about doctrine, discipline, or even current events. Those questions usually came from members or chapters who were struggling with a particular situation associated with a question about the Faith. In the beginning, and for many years, the task of answering those questions fell on the shoulders of the officers within CUF. They were the ones preparing position papers and evaluating catechism series and liturgical practices.

In 1997, CUF launched the Information Services (IS) department with a toll-free hotline (1-800-MY FAITH [693-2484]) open to anyone who had a question. Anyone, not just members, could call and answer any question about the Catholic Church. Under the leadership of Philip C. L. Gray serving as Director (1998-2001), IS became the largest and most active program within the CUF International Office. During those years, IS received an average of 800 requests and distributed more than 83,000 pages of material each month. By the end of 2001, IS had researched over 10,000 different questions and issues.

Regrettably, after Philip’s departure in September 2001 until CUF’s affiliation to The Saint Joseph Foundation (SJF) in February 2017, the IS department went into a decline. Virtually no new research occurred after about 2009. With the affiliation, Philip became the President of CUF and reinstated the IS department with its toll-free number and mission of answering any question about the Catholic Church. The previous research was recovered from the electronic files of CUF.

Today, IS receives questions from website and phone inquiries. Anyone may contact CUF and ask any question pertaining to the Catholic Church. If IS does not have an answer for you, the staff will research your question and provide you with answer. Answers are given in writing, either as a Faith Fact or as a research letter. This allows CUF to provide substantive magisterial and scriptural citations that you can reference later. CUF members may receive the answers in electronic form. All other inquirers will receive the answers by mail.

IS staff are available to take questions by phone from 8:00 am until 4:00 pm EST, Monday thru Friday. If you have a question about the Catholic Faith, call us at 1-800-MY-FAITH (693-2484) or contact us via our online form. Relying totally on donations, the service is offered free of charge.

Faith Facts

Faith Facts are the backbone of Information Services (IS).

Each Faith Fact provides an answer to a common question about the Catholic Church. A Faith Fact generally provides more information than a researched letter. Each Faith Fact is the culmination of many research letters and common questions about the Church. Each one provides a question, a summary answer, and an expanded discussion that includes context and application. CUF doesn’t just explain what the Church teaches or does, we also explain why and how to live the Faith. About half of IS inquiries are answered by a Faith Fact.
